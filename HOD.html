OFFICE OF THE DEPARTMENT

WELCOME

Dr Jamal Deen Abdulai

Jamal-Deen Abdulai is the Head of the Department of Computer Science, University of Ghana. He is an academic in computer science with over 10 years of working experience in both industry and academia. He graduated with PhD in Computer Science in 2009 and MPhil in 2006 from University of Glasgow, UK. Prior to that, he received BSc in Computer Science in 2002 from the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST).
