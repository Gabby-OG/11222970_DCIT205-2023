Computer Science Department

Welcome

"We are living in the era of Information Technology, with Computer Science as its backbone. To fully harness the potential of Information Technology and accelerate economic progress, understanding computer science is crucial. This field encompasses the creation and analysis of applications that facilitate our daily computer usage, the software systems that underpin these applications, and the strategies and techniques used to create both applications and systems that operate reliably, accurately, and ethically. Computer programming is a fundamental skill in Computer Science. Employers hiring Computer Scientists anticipate proficient programmers who are familiar with contemporary techniques and tools, and can apply solid engineering principles to the creation, development, and upkeep of computer programs."

The Department offers two undergraduate degree programmes, Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Single-Major (3:2:1:1) and Major-Minor (3:2:2:1) as well as Bachelor of Science in Information Technology.
